Dungeons and Dragons Adventure: Speaker of the Trees

As winter draws closer, the village of Ameroth grows desperate for help. Any help. They claim a ghost haunts their forest, and the villagers, terrified of this apparition and low on firewood, beg the nearest adventures for help. The problem is not so simple however. As remants of the work left behind by a lonely botantist come to light, it turns out, ghosts are the least of our adventerers' worries. Fight deadly beasts and blazing fires in: Speaker of the Trees.

Speaker of the Trees is no ordinary dungeon crawl. Through discussion with the villagers and the investigation of a certain deceased botanist, players will discover the true nature of the 'ghost' haunting the forest. In an exciting final encounter, players must team up with wildlife and awakened plants to put out the fires and defeat the elemental that threatens everyone.
